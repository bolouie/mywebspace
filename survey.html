Well-Being Survey

Online

  • The purpose of this online survey is to capture a multidimensional assessment of well-being from a gender identity perspective. It has been designed to focus on the inclusion of transgender, non-binary, and gender non-conforming adults.
  • This online survey consists of 14 questions and takes about 5 minutes to complete based on a modified version of the ICOPPE Scale.
  • The ICOPPE scale measures Interpersonal, Community, Occupational, Physical, Psychological, and Economic areas of life plus considers a subjective rating for overall wellness.
