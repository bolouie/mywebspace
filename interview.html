Semi-Structured

Interview

  • The purpose of the semi-structured interview is to explore well-being experiences from the unique perspective of transgender adults in the workplace. This will directly help generate a new map that supports the well-being of transgender adults working in frontline occupations such as healthcare, education/teaching, and public services.
  • These interviews are open to Canadian and international eligible participants. Initial contact by email.
  • Interview participants are welcome to take the online survey in addition to their participation in a semi-structured interview.

Interview sessions will be held virtually via Zoom. Strictly audio (mp3) recordings will be securely stored and transcribed for research purposes only.
